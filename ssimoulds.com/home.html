Leading Manufacturer of Plastic and Rubber Components

Autoson Moulds - Autoson Industries is a leading manufacturer of Automotive Plastic Products, Rubber Parts, Plastic Injection-Moulded Electronic Devices Parts, Plastic Household Products, Appliances Plastic Products and Fitting, Furniture Hardware Parts, Vacuum formed tray, Vacuum formed packaging, Custom Plastic Products.
