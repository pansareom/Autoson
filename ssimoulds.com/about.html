About Autoson Moulds - Autoson Industries

We emerged as an industry who believe in providing the global quality products to the end users at low manufacturing cost, we have the facility of in-house tooling, product designing, product development and production

Contact Us

Don't hesitate to send us a message!

Autoson Moulds is a leading manufacturer of automotive plastic components, consumer electronics plastic components, home appliances plastic products and plastic fittings, furniture hardware products, plastic vacuum formed and thermoformed products. Established two decades ago, the Company has demonstrated rapid growth and is recognised for low cost manufacturing capabilities and established high quality client base.

Autoson Moulds is capable of product designing, product development, turnkey manufacturing. we are globally competitive in terms of cost of production and high quality products.

Autoson Moulds continues to charge forward achieving excellence in manufacturing plastic products, rubber components and plastic vacuum formed products. We have developed more than 2800 plastic injection moulds and developed over 500 products and supplying to the most renowned companies in India and overseas companies also.

Autoson Moulds has been an prominent supplier of automotive majors like Minda Corporation Limited, Lumax Industries Limited, Neolite ZKW Lighting Limited; electronics majors like Ntl Electronics India Limited, HPL Electric and Power Private Limited, RC Bentex Energy Metering Private Limited; home appliances majors like Nutech Technoservices Private Limited, Padmini Impex Private Limited, Cata Appliances Limited as a preferred contract manufacturer.

Our Vision

To emerge as the largest and most cost effective manufacturer and supplier of automotive plastic components, home appliances Plastic Products and fittings, electronics devices plastic components, furniture hardware products for the domestic and international market.

Since the foundation of our company, Autoson Moulds is driven by its vision. Innovation and satisfaction are at the core of everything we do at Autoson Moulds, we intend to expand our business operations and develop innovative products at low cost. we will be focused towards becoming a preferred supplier at global OEM's and also emerge as a preferred employer in our industry and we always intend to increase the level of priority to make sure that only the best and tested products are supplied to our customers.

Our Mission
